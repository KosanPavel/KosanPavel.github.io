About Me

Data and Math enthusiast & Eternal Student

I’m a Data Professional with a deep interest in data science, engineering, machine learning, and mathematics. Professionally, I work as a Data and Analytics Engineer and Data Analyst.

My core professional focus includes building GCP BI/analytics infrastructure (ETL/ELT pipelines, data warehousing, AI analytics and automation), performing advanced customer analytics (RFM segmentation, Cohort analysis, CLV modeling and Customer 360), and developing machine learning models/systems such as recommendation models, customer churn prediction pipelines and so on.

Personally, I am driven by the intersection of comuper and data science and engineering, mathematics and statistics and AI. Lately, I have been diving deep into graph analytics, exploring technologies like Neo4j, PuppyGraph, Kuzu and the mathematical foundations of graph data analysis.

Outside of work and research, I enjoy large fantasy and sci-fi novels — and I'm currently writing one of my own.
